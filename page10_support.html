Frequently Asked Questions

How can I cancel my booking?

You can cancel your booking from the 'My Bookings' section in your account dashboard.

Can I reschedule my hotel stay?

Yes, depending on the hotel's policy. Contact support or check the reschedule option in your bookings.

Where can I view my confirmed bookings?

Login and go to your account to view all your confirmed and past bookings.
